Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Alfredo sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Boil water and cook pasta until tender.
  2. Season and cook chicken in a pan until golden.
  3. Melt butter and add garlic, then stir in Alfredo sauce.
  4. Mix pasta into the sauce.
  5. Add sliced chicken on top.
  6. Finish with parmesan cheese.
