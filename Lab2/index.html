Reverse

  1. Adjective

    1. opposite or contrary to a previous or normal condition
    2. acting, operating, or arranged in a manner contrary to the usual
    3. so made that the part which normally prints in color appears white against a colored background

  2. Verb

    1. to turn completely about in position or direction
    2. to turn upside down
    3. to cause to take an opposite point of view

  3. Noun

    1. something directly contrary to something else
    2. an act or instance of reversing
    3. the back part of something
